We're much more than tours and excursions; at Reef Tours & Travel, we take you beyond the resort and into the heart of the island where setting sail, grabbing the reins and tasting rich flavors are the every day for us that become the lasting memories for you. We are an independent operator of authentic, curated excursions and experiences in Tanzania. Our amazing company includes a dedicated team of multi-language private tour guides, sea-mates, adventure-seekers, and experiential-masters who spotlight only the best-of-the-best expeditions and destinations for everyone according to the resort/hotel they are staying. Operating in 9 destination – Zanzibar, Dar es Salaam, Selous National Park, Serengeti National Park, Ngorongoro Conservation Area, Manyara National Park, Tarangire National Park, Mount Kilimanjaro, and Mikumi National Park; we know our country inside and out, and it's our privilege to share it with you.
