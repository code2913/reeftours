Spice Tour

The Spice Tour is one of the most popular excursions in Zanzibar. Zanzibar was one of the worldâ€™s leading producers of spices such as clove, nutmeg and cinnamon. Spice tour takes you to an organic farm with a wide variety of plants around the year.

Three hours tour through the spice farm plantation. This involves visiting the spices growing areas to see different spices and to learn their nature, uses and taste some of them right on the spot depending on the season where some are used medicinal, cosmetics, ornamental plants and herbs.

Cloves, nutmeg, rambutan, lipstick fruit, iodine plant, lemongrass, vanilla, henna, shampoo plant etc. Our spice tour offers you the opportunity to indulge your senses with a kaleidoscope of smells, tastes and textures. From the punch of a chilli to the refreshing succulence of green coconut flesh, we promise that this tour will awaken your senses. It also provides you with the unique opportunity to purchase these exclusive spices straight from the source.

Useful information
