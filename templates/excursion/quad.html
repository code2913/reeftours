Before leaving, you will receive a brief briefing, information and procedures from professional and experienced instructors. In order to participate in the unforgettable experience, you must have a driver's license.

Discover the most authentic places on the island and enjoy field trips and views that include:

  1. A meeting with the children of the villages and houses made of red earth and mud.
  2. Baobab trees, fruit plantations and coconut trees, sugarcane plantations, pineapples and rice fields.
  3. An overview and historical background of Zanzibar from the guide.

Tour Includes
  • Private transportation.
  • Helmet (must be worn at all times when riding)•
  • Safety briefing prior to departure
  • Introduction to Quads & explanation of controls
  • Practice ride to assess ability
  • Professional tuition at all times
  • Visiting two villages and cave
  • Mineral water
Safety First!
  • Only persons over 18 years old can drive a quad (Kids over 8 years may take a part in a tour as a passenger)
  • You are obliged to wear a helmet
  • It is forbidden to overtake the guide or other participants of the trip
  • Keep safety distance between the quads
  • Always keep two hands on a handlebar. Do not take the photos during the ride. You will have plenty of stops to take a picture
  • NOTE: This is not an extreme ride, no jumping or speeding.
  • Any clients suspected of being under the influence of alcohol or drugs will not be allowed to do this activity
