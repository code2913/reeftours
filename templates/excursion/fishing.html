We organize a half day Zanzibar fishing charter which is the perfect way to Escape your Hotel and spend a Morning or an Afternoon looking for some Adrenaline filled Fishing Action! Join us onboard for a few hours of Fishing Fun out on the warm tropical waters of Zanzibar!
Game Fishing Trip

During our Half Day Zanzibar Fishing Charter, we focus on the fishing areas of Leven Bank (which lies 6 miles north of Nungwi), the Zanzibar Channel and the north east coast of Zanzibar Island. Of course, if it is quite inshore, we will head offshore in search of better fishing should time allow during the course of your Fishing Charter.

Inclusions
  • Private transportation.
  • Captain
  • Deckhand
  • Trolling Rods & Reels
  • A selection of Artificial Lures
  • Terminal Tackle
  • Bait
  • Tropical Fruits
  • Snacks
  • Sodas
  • Mineral Water
Charter Duration: 5 Hours

Morning Charter Times: 7am – 12pm

Afternoon Charter Times: 1pm – 6pm.

Meeting point

Our representative will meet you at the hotel lobby at the arranged time.

Departs from

Nungwi Beach, north Zanzibar.

Live tour guide

We offer this tour in English, French, Spanish, and Italian.

For any other nationalities please advise when sending your inquiry so we can make an arrangement for you
